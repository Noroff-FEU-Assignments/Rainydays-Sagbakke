Web Security

How would you advise a potential client to secure his/her website from web security attacks? Note: the word count for your answer should not be greater than 250 words

When it comes to web security, we have 6 points for best practice that we follow. Here are some of them: Sanszination of data-In short, it's about data that the systems accept coming in the right format. We do this either manually or with the help of a third-party solution. Secure authorization and authentication-Be sure that authorization and authentication are challenging to hack. This can be solved by manual work or with an identity and access manager. Such as OneLogin. Make use of a well-known third-party application-Test third-party providers before putting it live. Read their testimonial and what experiences they have with the service or product.
